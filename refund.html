Cancellation & Refund Policy

How to cancel the optional ad-removal subscription and request a refund.

Effective: 19 July 2026 · Last updated: 19 July 2026

This template is provided for product launch preparation and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified legal professional in your jurisdiction.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the optional HabitFine ad-removal subscription purchased through Google Play.

2. Cancellation

You can cancel through Google Play: Play Store → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → HabitFine → Cancel. Cancellation normally stops future renewal; access may continue until the paid period ends.

3. Refund requests

Payments are processed by Google Play. Refund eligibility, timing and processing are generally governed by Google Play’s refund policy and applicable consumer law. Submit requests through your Google Play order history or Google support.

4. Core features

The subscription is intended only to remove ads. Cancelling does not remove local habits or lock core tracking features.

5. Duplicate or unauthorized purchases

Report suspected unauthorized charges to Google Play promptly and contact us with the order date and non-sensitive order reference. Never send full payment-card details.
