Privacy Policy

How HabitFine handles local app data, ads, purchases, support messages and shared reports.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how HabitFine handles information when you use the Android application and this website.

2. Information stored locally

The app stores habits, check-ins, penalties, analytics, reminder settings, profile name, selected avatar and other preferences on your device. A selected profile photo is accessed through an Android-provided local URI. HabitFine does not operate a cloud habit database in version 1.

3. Information we may receive

If you contact support, we receive the information you include in your email. If ads are enabled, Google AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising information, approximate location derived from IP, diagnostics and interaction data under Google’s policies. Google Play processes purchases and subscription status.

4. Sharing and exports

HabitFine creates a report image only when you request it. Android’s share sheet determines the destination. Backup files are created or restored only through a location you choose. We do not sell personal information.

5. Third-party services

The app may use Google Play Services, Google AdMob and Google Play Billing. Their processing is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Retention and deletion

Local data remains until you reset the app, clear storage, restore another backup, or uninstall. Support emails remain as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain records. See Data deletion.

7. Children

HabitFine is not directed to children under 13. If local law requires a higher minimum age, that higher age applies.

8. Security

We use a local-first design to reduce data transfer. No storage method is completely secure; protect your device, backups and shared images.

9. Your choices

You can decline notification permission, reset local data, avoid selecting a photo, avoid sharing reports, and manage ad personalization through Android or Google settings where available.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the product changes. The revised effective date will be shown on this page.
