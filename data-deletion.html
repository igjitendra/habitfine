Data Deletion

Steps to remove local app data, backups, shared images and support communications.

Effective: 19 July 2026 · Last updated: 19 July 2026

1. Reset inside HabitFine2. Clear Android app storage3. Uninstall4. Delete exported files5. Support communications
This template is provided for product launch preparation and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified legal professional in your jurisdiction.

1. Reset inside HabitFine

Open HabitFine → Settings → Reset all local data. Confirm the warning. This removes habits, logs and analytics stored by the app.

2. Clear Android app storage

Open Android Settings → Apps → HabitFine → Storage → Clear storage. Menu names vary by manufacturer.

3. Uninstall

Uninstalling normally removes app-private local data. Backup JSON files and shared images saved elsewhere are not automatically deleted.

4. Delete exported files

Use your file manager to delete HabitFine backup JSON files and report images from locations where you saved or shared them.

5. Support communications

To request deletion of information you sent directly by email, contact jitendraeditiz@gmail.com from the same address.
